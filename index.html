/ Background

The actuarial edge

Before BI, I worked as an actuary — using mathematics, statistics, and financial theory to measure and manage risk, and to put a price on an uncertain future. That discipline — rigorous math, honest assumptions, defensible models — is the backbone of every system I build today.

// The Equivalence Principle

EPV(Premiums) = EPV(Benefits) + EPV(Expenses) + Profit

EPV = Expected Present Value — today's value of uncertain future cash flows. Price = expected cost of risk + time value of money + expenses + profit.

// Pricing in action — 1-yr term life
